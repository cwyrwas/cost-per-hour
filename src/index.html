75% to 90% of your revenue comes from services.

Do you know what delivering one hour of service costs? Sadly, most owners don't.

That means service prices are based on either guesswork or what competing salons/spa/medspas are charging.

Why charge time?

  • Except for retail, we are in the business of selling time.
  • Charging for time puts value back into skill and expertise.
  • Everything, including professional products, is included in the time quoted.
  • Drastically simplify your service menus.
  • It’s time to charge for your time as other professionals do!

What does charging by time mean?

  • It drives efficiency and productivity because the focus is now on time.
  • It's easy to understand and break it down into 45, 30, and 15-minute price/time increments.
  • Higher revenues because nothing slips through the cracks.
  • Train and coach service providers to work within time standards.
  • It simplifies and reduces miss-quoting prices on the phone or during consultations. For example, “This service requires two hours. We charge $XX per hour.”
  • Yes, the processing time is included in the time fee.

Imagine reinforcing your brand using a pricing structure that charges for time, skills, and expertise rather than the "name" of specific services.
